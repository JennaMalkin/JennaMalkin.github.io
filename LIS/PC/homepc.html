Working at Pearl Cove

Welcome to Pearl Cove!

The beautiful Shire of Pearl Cove stretches from Golden Waters in the north to Thimley Bay in the south. Inland the Shire encompasses Bourke's Vale and the township of Tolleville.

Library services are centrally located in the popular holiday resort of Pearl Cove.

Our clients include young families, retirees, students, professionals and the holidaymakers who make Pearl Cove such a vibrant summer location.

Our resources cover the diverse needs of our population and include collections in: leisure reading, local business and local area information, as well as strong collection of non-fiction covering the needs of school-aged children, tertiary students and the general interests of our residents.

Pearl Cove Library is committed to meeting the information and leisure needs of our clients in a variety of media: as well as books, magazines and newspapers you can enjoy browsing the internet, borrowing the latest CDs or DVDs, or utilising our extensive database subscriptions.

And of course our close connection to Pearl Cove Council means we have current information about almost everything happening in the Shire.
